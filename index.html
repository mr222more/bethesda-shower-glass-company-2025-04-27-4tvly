Our Story

Founded in 2010, Bethesda Glass Company emerged from a passion for combining European craftsmanship with innovative American design. Our journey began when founder Michael Roberts discovered a gap in the market for premium, space-efficient shower solutions.

Today, we're driven by our mission to transform bathrooms into sophisticated spaces through superior glass technology and unparalleled customer service. Our core values of innovation, quality, and sustainability guide every project we undertake.

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes your glass different from standard shower glass?

Our premium European glass features advanced texturing technology, superior thickness (8-10mm), and proprietary slip-resistant treatment, offering unmatched safety and durability.

How long does installation typically take?

Most installations are completed within 1-2 days, with minimal disruption to your home. Our certified technicians ensure precise fitting and proper sealing.

What maintenance is required?

Our glass requires minimal maintenance - simple cleaning with standard glass cleaner is sufficient. The special coating resists water spots and mineral buildup.

Do you offer custom sizes and designs?

Yes, we specialize in custom solutions. Each installation is tailored to your specific space and design preferences.

What warranty coverage do you provide?

Our comprehensive 10-year warranty covers materials, hardware, and installation. We also offer lifetime support for all our products.
